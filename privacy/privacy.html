1. Overview

Starry Systems is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, usage, and storage. Our operations are guided by the principle of data minimization and strict adherence to international privacy standards.

We do not collect, store, share, or process any personal information. Our website is designed to operate without the need for user data, cookies, accounts, or tracking technologies.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect or request any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, or other contact information
  • IP addresses or geolocation data
  • Device, browser, or usage information
  • User-submitted content or uploaded files
  • Financial information or payment data

No forms, registrations, or input fields on our website require or allow users to provide personal data.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Starry Systems does not use cookies, web beacons, tracking pixels, analytics tools, or any client-side or server-side tracking mechanisms.

There are no third-party scripts, embedded advertisements, analytics services, or social media trackers on our website.

4. User Accounts

We do not offer or support user accounts, login functionality, or any form of user identity management. Users cannot register, sign in, or maintain profiles on our platform.

5. E-Commerce and Financial Data

We do not conduct any online sales or financial transactions. Our website does not process, store, or transmit any payment information, including credit card details, billing addresses, or order histories.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website does not integrate with third-party platforms in any way that transmits or processes user data. Any external links provided are for informational purposes only. Starry Systems is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal data, our website is compliant with all relevant child privacy regulations, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

8. Data Security

Although no personal data is collected, Starry Systems maintains enterprise-grade security controls to protect its infrastructure, including:

  • Full HTTPS encryption (TLS 1.3)
  • Secure server environments
  • DNSSEC-enabled domains
  • Active firewall protection and intrusion prevention
  • Regular updates and vulnerability patching

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically to remain informed of our practices.
